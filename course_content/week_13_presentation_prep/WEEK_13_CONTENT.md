# Week 13: Final Presentation Preparation

## Overview
**Theme:** Crafting the Story
**Duration:** Thirteenth week of semester
**Deliverables:** Draft Final Presentation, Peer Review Feedback, Revised Presentation

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this week, students will be able to:
1. Structure a comprehensive final presentation
2. Design slides that communicate effectively
3. Give and receive constructive peer feedback
4. Iterate based on feedback

---

## Content to Create

### Video 1: Final Presentation Structure (10 min)

**Key Topics:**
- Full presentation structure (30-40 min + Q&A)
- Section breakdown:

```
1. Executive Summary (3 min)
   - Key findings in 3 bullets
   - Top recommendations

2. Project Context (2 min)
   - AWG background (brief refresh)
   - Research questions

3. Data & Methodology (5 min)
   - Data sources
   - Key transformation (unit normalization)
   - Analytical approaches

4. Findings by RQ (15 min)
   - RQ1: Price Gap (3 min)
   - RQ2: Pass-Through (2 min)
   - RQ3: Elasticity (2 min)
   - RQ4: Cross-Brand (2 min)
   - RQ5: Geographic (2 min)
   - RQ6: Tariffs (2 min)
   - Integration (2 min)

5. Strategic Recommendations (5 min)
   - Prioritized actions
   - Expected impact
   - Implementation considerations

6. Limitations & Future Work (2 min)
   - What we couldn't answer
   - Suggestions for next steps

7. Q&A (10-15 min)

8. Appendix (reference only)
   - Detailed methodology
   - Additional charts
   - Data quality details
```

**Animation Ideas:**
- Presentation timeline
- Section cards with time allocations

### Video 2: Visual Design Tips (8 min)

**Key Topics:**
- Slide design principles
- Chart selection guide:
  | Message | Chart Type |
  |---------|------------|
  | Distribution | Histogram |
  | Comparison | Bar chart |
  | Trend | Line chart |
  | Relationship | Scatter plot |
  | Composition | Pie/Stacked bar |
  | Geographic | Map/Heatmap |

- Color usage: Consistent palette, meaningful colors
- Font hierarchy: Title (24pt+), Body (18pt+), Notes (14pt)
- The "squint test": Main message visible from across the room

**Animation Ideas:**
- Before/after slide makeovers
- Chart selection decision tree

---

### Video 3: Peer Review Process (5 min)

**Key Topics:**
- Giving feedback: Specific, actionable, balanced
- Receiving feedback: Listen, don't defend, clarify
- Focus areas: Content accuracy, visual clarity, flow, timing
- Using the rubric effectively

**Peer Review Timeline:**
- Day 1-2: Complete draft presentation
- Day 3: Exchange with review partner
- Day 4: Provide written feedback
- Day 5-7: Revise based on feedback
